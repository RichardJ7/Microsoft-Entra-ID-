# Summary 

This lab focused on the Microsoft Entra ID feature Identity Protection. Identity Protection was designed to monitor, detect and remediate risks related to identity. Through the monitoring of a logins, Identity Protection provides the ability to customize policies based on identity risks. In this lab, I configured two different policies to implement in an environment to mitigate identity risks related to sign-ins and users.
